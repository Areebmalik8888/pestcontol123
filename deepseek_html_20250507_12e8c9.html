Our Mission

The Beauty Kind Foundation is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization dedicated to providing free pest control services to low-income families, elderly residents, and other vulnerable populations throughout the United States.

Founded in 2015, we believe everyone deserves to live in a safe, healthy environment free from pests that can cause health problems and property damage.

Our Services

Residential Pest Control

Free treatment for common household pests including rodents, cockroaches, bed bugs, ants, and more for qualifying households.

Community Education

Workshops and materials on pest prevention and safe, environmentally-friendly pest management practices.

Emergency Services

Rapid response for severe infestations that pose immediate health risks to vulnerable populations.
